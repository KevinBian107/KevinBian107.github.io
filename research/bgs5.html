Efficient Naturalistic Long Horizon Sequencing & Generation

Since naturalistic behaviors are, in nature, to be long horizon, this long horizon sequence modeling problem would be particularly challenging as we want to model long horizon sequences that needs to have biomechanically realistic movements.

To this end, advised by professor Scott Linderman at Stanford University, I am doing sequence modeling on the latent space generated by Mimic-mjx, which abstracts away the "physics" part of the project and provide a biomechanically realistic embedding for developing sequence models (i.e. deep state-space models) upon. Particularly, I am building upon previous works in the lab (S5) for an long sequence or naturalistic behavior generative model that is efficient to train.
