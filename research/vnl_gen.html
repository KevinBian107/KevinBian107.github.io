State-Space Models Tailored For Biomechanically Realistic Behavior Generation

I am applying State-Space Models (SSM) - S4 and S5 - tailored to latent data generated by Mimic-MJX for biomechanically realistic behavior sequence dynamics modeling and generation via latent optimization in professor Scott Linderman's lab at Stanford University.
