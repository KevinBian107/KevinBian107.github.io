More Beyond Suboptimum

I am currently researching on the theoretical formulation of Continual Reinforcment Learning with my team and advising from Prof. Sicun Gao in UCSD's Computer Science & Engineering Department. We are focusing on thinking about what these RL algorithms are actually doing on a high dimensional space and exploring the benifits of incorporating notions of sub-optimality using the probabilistic inference lens of controls inference.
