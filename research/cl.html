Learning Learning is Planning

I am currently researching on Meta-Planning as Inference (MPI), which is a theoretical formulation in the intersection of Reinforcment Learning & Meta Learning using an Probabilistic & Latent Inference perspective with my team and advising from Professor Ying Nian Wu from UCLA Statistics & Data Science Department (we previously recieved advising from Professor Sicun Gao from UCSD's Computer Science & Engineering Department and dived into this problem from an search & optimization perspective). We believe that probability itself carries certain "structure" that can help us systematically understand different environment and solve the multi-planning tasks.
