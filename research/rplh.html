Hellucinating Multi-agent Language Model

Cover page image borrowed from Prof Sichun Gao from UCSD CSE.

System Documentation

Large Language Models have remarkable in-context learning abilities. We hope to build agent that is caplable of social-reasoning and expecting what the other agent should be doing. We are building a generalize world model for every single agent in this environment and hopefully moving a step closer to level-one agent. Here is a easy schematic over view of our work:

Level-1 Agent Concept (borrowed from Prof Zhiting Hu from UCSD HDSI)

Particularly, we are developing frameworks for multi-agent collaborations using Role-Playing Leader-Hellucinating LLM, or in short, RPLH. Here we illustrate an rendering of our RPLH system during firt iteration where the central (HCA) agent hallucinate about future plans to execute and the consequences of according plans.
