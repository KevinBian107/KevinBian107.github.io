Hellucinating Multi-agent Language Model

We hope to build agent that is caplable of Social-reasoning and expecting what the other agent should be doing. We are building a generalize world model for every single agent in this environment and hopefully moving a step closer to Level-one agent. Here is a easy schematic over view of our work:

Clean schematic of RPLH-LLM

Particularly, we are developing frameworks for multi-agent collaborations using Role-Playing Leader-Hellucinating LLM, or in short, RPLH-LLM. We are currently developing on minimal example and we are planning to adapt to MCTS fine-tuned LLM with VirtualHome later. Here is an example of agents communicating using a box move example followng this ICRA-2024 Paper:

Considering these points:
