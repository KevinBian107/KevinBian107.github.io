Many recommenders have been built from an user's perspective for reconmmending products. However, little has done from the business owner's perspective. We constructed a hybrid recommender system with collaborative filters and content-based filtering. The model will use features of locations, the interaction of location and user, and the temporal evolution of the interaction to predict a user's rating of a business.
